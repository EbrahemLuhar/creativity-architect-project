Pemberton Hospital Ward

A design for a five storey extension to the existing ward.

The facility would potentially include research laboratories, café, seating area and consulting rooms, WC facilities and project hall changing facilities. The design incorporated a five storey multi-functional public atrium space as an enterence with integrated meeting facilities and catering.
